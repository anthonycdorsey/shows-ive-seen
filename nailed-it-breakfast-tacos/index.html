Nailed It Breakfast Tacos

Do you like breakfast tacos? Do you like when people look at you and say, "Wow! You really NAILED-it!"? If yes, make these. (Note: these are tacos, not burritos).

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Jimmy Dean Sausage Key: Ensure meat is fresh and loose (no casings).
  • 6 Slices Smoked Bacon
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 2 Small White Potatoes, Cubed
  • 1 Small Onion, Chopped
  • 1 Small Red Bell Pepper (or Poblano)
  • 1 Large Jalapeño, Chopped
  • 1/2 Cup Cilantro, Chopped
  • 1 Tbsp Fiesta Chili Powder
  • 1 Tsp Fiesta Cumin
  • 1 Tsp Fiesta Oregano
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt & Black Pepper
  • 4 Eggs, Beaten
  • 2 Cups Sharp Cheddar, Shredded
  • 8 Flour Tortillas (4.5" or 6") Must use flour. No exceptions.
  • Salsa (Super fresh is best)

Instructions

The Base: Cook sausage in a medium skillet over medium-high heat. Combine all spices in a small bowl. Remove meat but keep the grease. Cook bacon until crisp, remove, and allow both to cool.

The Aromatics: In the remaining grease, brown the garlic. Add potatoes and cook until browned and crisp (5-10 mins). Chop the bacon while you wait.

The Mix: Add onion, bell pepper, jalapeño, and spice mixture to the potatoes. Cook until translucent. Re-add the sausage and bacon.

The Secret: Add beaten eggs. Do not stir too much! Allow them to cook into visible, small chunks. Top with cheese and cilantro, cover with a lid, and let it melt completely.

The Finish: Heat flour tortillas directly over a gas burner or wrapped in foil in a 350˚ oven. Spoon in the mixture, top with salsa, and immediately welcome everyone to NAILED-it!

Serves 4 / Two tacos per person / — Anthony C. Dorsey Archive —
