Nailed It Breakfast Tacos

Do you like breakfast tacos? Do you like when people look at you and say, "Wow! You really NAILED-it!"? (Note: these are tacos, not burritos).

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Jimmy Dean Sausage Key: Ensure meat is fresh and loose (no casings).
  • 6 Slices Smoked Bacon
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 2 Small White Potatoes
  • 1 Small Onion, Chopped
  • 1 Small Red Bell Pepper (or Poblano)
  • 1 Large Jalapeño, Chopped
  • 1/2 Cup Cilantro, Chopped
  • 1 Tbsp Fiesta Chili Powder
  • 1 Tsp Fiesta Cumin
  • 1 Tsp Fiesta Oregano
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt & Black Pepper
  • 4 Eggs, Beaten
  • 2 Cups Sharp Cheddar, Shredded
  • 8 Flour Tortillas (4.5" or 6") Must use flour. No exceptions.
  • Salsa (Super fresh is best, ask me for my recipes!)

Instructions

Step 1: Cook sausage in a skillet over medium-high heat. Combine spices in a small bowl. Remove meat, leaving grease. Cook bacon until crisp, remove, and allow to cool.

Step 2: In the grease, brown the minced garlic. Add potatoes and cook until browned and crisp (5-10 mins). Chop the bacon while you wait.

Step 3: Add onion, bell pepper, jalapeño, and spice mixture. Cook until translucent. Add sausage and bacon back in.

Step 4: Add beaten eggs. Do not stir too much! Eggs should be in visible chunks. Top with cheese and cilantro, cover with a lid to melt.

Step 5: Heat tortillas over a gas burner or wrapped in foil in a 350˚ oven. Spoon in the mixture, top with salsa, and welcome everyone to NAILED-it!
