Shows I've Seen

I’ve kept a ticket stub from every concert I’ve attended. This is a running archive of live performance. A record of what I chose to spend time with, across years, genres, and cities.
— This is a growing archive. New stubs and memories added as they surface. —

The Wallflowers (1997)

Frank Erwin Center, Austin, TX. First arena show. The scale of the sound felt massive at 14.

Blur (1997)

Liberty Lunch, Austin, TX. Liberty Lunch was at its peak. Small club energy for a world-class band.

Aerosmith (1997)

Frank Erwin Center, Austin, TX. A masterclass in stage presence. The scarves on the mic stand were iconic.

Third Eye Blind (1998)

Austin Music Hall, Austin, TX. The venue is gone now, but the heat of that April night remains vivid.
